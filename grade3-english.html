Blooming Kids House: Grade 3 – English Language Arts Test

Time Remaining: 30:00

The Lost Kitten

Sam found a kitten in his yard. It was small and fluffy with gray fur. He gave it milk and a soft blanket. The kitten purred and stayed close to Sam. The next day, Sam made posters to find the kitten’s owner. He wrote, “Found: Gray kitten near Oak Street. Call Sam.” Use the passage to answer the questions below.

1. What is the main idea of the passage?

2. What does the word “purred” mean in the passage?

3. Why did Sam make posters?

4. What did Sam give the kitten?

5. Which word best describes Sam?

6. What happened after Sam gave the kitten milk and a blanket?

Lila’s Story

Lila wrote a story about her dog, Max. Here is a part of her story: “Max run fast in the park. He chase a ball and bring it back to me. I loves Max.” Use the passage to answer the questions below.

7. Which sentence has a grammar mistake?

8. How should “Max run fast in the park” be corrected?

9. How should “I loves Max” be corrected?

10. Which word in the story is a verb?

11. What is the best way to combine these sentences: “He chase a ball. He bring it back to me.”?

12. Which word should replace “fast” to make the story more descriptive?

13. Which sentence is written correctly?

14. What is the meaning of the word “brave” in the sentence: “The brave firefighter saved the cat”?

15. Which word is a synonym for “big”?

16. Which sentence uses a comma correctly?

17. What is the correct plural form of “child”?

18. Which sentence is in the past tense?

19. What is the best topic sentence for a paragraph about a fun day at the park?

20. Which word is an adjective in the sentence: “The shiny red apple fell from the tree”?

21. Which sentence uses the correct form of “their”?

22. What is the prefix in the word “unhappy”?

23. Which sentence is a question?

24. Which word should be capitalized in the sentence: “i went to paris with my family”?

25. What is the main purpose of a story about a talking animal?

26. Which sentence uses quotation marks correctly?

27. Which word is a noun in the sentence: “The bird sings a sweet song”?

28. What is the suffix in the word “kindness”?

29. Which sentence is an example of a complete sentence?

30. What is the best closing sentence for a letter to a friend?
