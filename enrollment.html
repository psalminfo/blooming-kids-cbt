Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring programs and services.

What grades do you tutor? +
We tutor students from Preschool all the way through Grade 12, covering all major subjects in the curriculum including Math, English, Sciences, and more.
How are sessions scheduled? +
You can choose from three schedules: Twice weekly (2 sessions), Thrice weekly (3 sessions), or Daily (5 sessions). Sessions are 1 hour each and scheduled at times convenient for you.
What is included in the tuition fee? +
The base fee includes 2 subjects. Additional subjects can be added at â‚¦40,000 each per month. All learning materials are provided. Fees are prorated based on start date.
Are the classes one-to-one? +
Yes. All our classes are 100% one-to-one, tailored to your child's learning needs. Each student receives personalized attention from their dedicated tutor.
Which platform do you use for lessons? +
Live classes are held on Zoom. Homework and class activities are managed via Google Classroom. Both platforms are easy to use and accessible on any device.
How do parents track progress? +
Parents have access to a Parent Portal, where you receive monthly feedback and detailed learning reports. You can also communicate directly with your child's tutor.
How is payment done? +
Payments are made in Naira. Fees depend on your child's grade level and number of classes per week. We accept bank transfers and provide payment receipts.
Are your tutors experienced and professional? +
Yes. All our tutors are experienced, trained, and professional educators, carefully vetted to deliver quality learning. They undergo continuous training and assessment.

Yes. We offer a flat discount of â‚¦10,000 for families enrolling more than one student. This discount is applied once per family, making our programs more affordable for siblings.
Can my child start mid-month? +
Absolutely! We prorate fees based on the start date, so you only pay for the sessions your child will attend. You can start anytime that works for you.
