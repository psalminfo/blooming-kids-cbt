Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What grades do you tutor?

+
We tutor students from Preschool all the way through Grade 12, covering all major subjects in the curriculum.

How are sessions scheduled?

+
You can choose from three schedules: Twice weekly (2 sessions), Thrice weekly (3 sessions), or Daily (5 sessions). Sessions are 1 hour each.

What is included in the tuition fee?

+
The base fee includes 2 subjects. Additional subjects can be added at ₦40,000 each per month. All learning materials are provided.

Are the classes one-to-one?

+
Yes. All our classes are 100% one-to-one, tailored to your child's learning needs.

Which platform do you use for lessons?

+
Live classes are held on Zoom. Homework and class activities are managed via Google Classroom.

How do parents track progress?

+
Parents have access to a Parent Portal, where you receive monthly feedback and detailed learning reports.

How is payment done?

+
Payments are made in Naira. Fees depend on your child's grade level and number of classes per week.

Are your tutors experienced and professional?

+
Yes. All our tutors are experienced, trained, and professional educators, carefully vetted to deliver quality learning.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

+
Yes. We offer a flat discount of ₦10,000 for families enrolling more than one student. This discount is applied once per family, making our programs more affordable for siblings.

Can my child start mid-month?

+
Absolutely! We prorate fees based on the start date, so you only pay for the sessions your child will attend.
