Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Blooming Kids House. We operate a multi-portal educational platform designed to help children blossom. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our students, parents, and tutors in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDP Act), GDPR, and COPPA.

2. Data Collection Across Portals

We collect information necessary to provide our educational services:

  • Students: Names, grade levels, and academic performance (test scores and responses).
  • Parents: Contact details (email/phone), and referral tracking data.
  • Tutors: Professional profiles, login credentials, and session management logs.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Tracking student academic growth and generating reports.
  • Facilitating secure communication between tutors and parents.
  • Managing the Referral Reward System for parents.
  • Note: We do NOT sell or rent your data to third-party advertisers.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Protecting the privacy of young learners is our priority. We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from students under the age of 13. Parents have the absolute right to review or request the deletion of their child's data at any time.

5. Data Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for secure data storage. Access to sensitive data is restricted via Firestore Security Rules, ensuring that tutors and management can only access information relevant to their roles.

6. Your Rights

Under the NDP Act and GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your stored data.
  • Correct any inaccurate information.
  • Request the permanent deletion of your account and data.
